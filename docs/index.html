The lazy eye exercise tool

This game is intended to help people with "Lazy eye" condition train their weaker eye. Amblyopia, commonly known as lazy eye, is the eye condition noted by reduced vision not correctable by glasses or contact lenses and is not due to any eye disease. The brain, for some reason, does not fully acknowledge the images seen by the amblyopic eye. This almost always affects only one eye but may manifest with reduction of vision in both eyes. It is estimated that three percent of children under six have some form of amblyopia.

How does it work?

Recent studies have shown that playing blocks games in a dichoptic way (when the eyes see different images) requires information to be sent to both eyes, making them work cooperatively. By making both eyes cooperate, the amblyopic brain is able to relearn as a result of an increase in the level of plasticity in the brain.

How to use it?

Before you begin, consult with your eye doctor

Prerequisites

  • You will need anaglyph glasses. You can buy them almost everywhere on the internet (try aliexpress, ebay or amazon)
  • For a productive session you'll have to configure colors accurately - so that your eyes will be working in a "dichoptic" way. way. Check out help page

General info

  • It is recommended (check this with your eye doctor) to play this game from 20 to 60 minutes a day in order to achieve some noticeable effect. It is possible to split this time into two or three sessions a day
  • Don't forget to rest and give your brain time to adapt
  • It is easier to start with a smaller screen first. The number of rows and columns of game field is adjustable, so you can play on various kinds of screens
  • If you feel it is too easy or too hard for you, you can try switching colors between your healthy and your lazy eye
