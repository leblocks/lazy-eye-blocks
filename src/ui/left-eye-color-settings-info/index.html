Left eye color setup:

  1. Put up your anaglyph glasses
  2. Close your right eye
  3. You can select color by clicking\tapping on an appropriate rectangle on the palette
  4. Selected color will appear on the rectangle that is above the palette
  5. Select shade of color so it would be invisible for your left eye
  6. Open your right eye and close left one
  7. Check that you can see selected color with your right eye
  8. Repeat this process until selected color will be visible for your left eye and invisible (almost is good to) for your right eye
