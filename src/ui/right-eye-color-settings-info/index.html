Right eye color setup:

  1. Put up your anaglyph glasses
  2. Close your left eye
  3. You can select color by clicking\tapping on an appropriate rectangle on the palette
  4. Selected color will appear on the rectangle that is above the palette
  5. Select shade of color so it would be invisible for your right eye
  6. Open your left eye and close right one
  7. Check that you can see selected color with your left eye
  8. Repeat this process until selected color will be visible for your right eye and invisible (almost is good to) for your left eye
